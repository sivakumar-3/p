Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Sahaya Joseph Idin, a passionate MERN stack developer and a college student with a strong drive to create innovative web applications. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a keen interest in web development, and the MERN stack has become my area of expertise.

I have dedicated countless hours to learning and honing my skills in MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js, which have become the foundation of my development journey.
With a solid understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I have been able to design and implement intuitive user interfaces that prioritize both aesthetics and usability. I am constantly seeking out new challenges and pushing myself to expand my skill set.
